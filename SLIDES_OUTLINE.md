# Slide Outline (10 slides)

1. **Title** - Team, project name, objective
2. **Problem** - Why this app and who benefits
3. **Architecture** - Frontend + API1 + API2 + DB1 + DB2 diagram
4. **Network Isolation** - Allowed vs forbidden communication matrix
5. **Docker Strategy** - Pinned images, non-root, healthchecks
6. **Persistence & Hot Reload** - Named volumes + bind mount proof
7. **CI/CD Security** - Trivy gate + build/push pipeline
8. **Teamwork** - Branching, reviews, ownership split
9. **Live Demo Results** - Screenshots or terminal snippets
10. **Challenges + Next Steps** - Lessons learned + future improvements
